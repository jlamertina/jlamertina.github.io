Pittsburgh, My Home Town

Walk with me on the streets of South Side, a neighborhood in the city of Pittsburgh, my home town...

Create your own web site on some unique topic. For example, tell the story of the neighbood where you grew up, a personal hobby or sport that you play, show pictures of historic buildings in your favorite city, display images of your friends, share your family -- basically anything of interest to you, that might also be of interest to others.

    Your web site must include:
  • At least two linked html files
  • A photo of you in the header
  • Multiple other images
  • A common style sheet
    Other details:
  • All files must be in one folder
  • Your html files will share a common header, footer, style, and navigation section
  • View your html files in a browser, checking that your navigation links work, and that all of your images appear.
  • Publish your web site:
    • Make sure that your home page file is named "index.html"
    • Login to your GitHub account
    • Create new repository, jsmith.github.io, replacing jsmith with your GitHub username
    • Upload your website HTML, CSS and image files (which may take a few minutes)
    • Open a new browser window, view your site online at http://jsmith.github.io
